[US] Winter Way Surfers

Hey there, Silvers! Today, July 9th, we logged onto Club Penguin Rewritten for our invasion of Winter Way and Cart Surfing Tournament. We successfully invaded our new land by showing off cool tactics and clean formations in the Stadium, Iceberg and inside Mine. Thank you Electrumm and Ryan for leading today’s event, and to every Silver that attended!

MAX: 19

Thank you everyone who attended the invasion today as well as participating in the cart surfing tournament we had afterwards! Congrats to Ryan for winning the tournament!

What are YOUR two cents? Did you guys have more fun at the invasion or cart surfing? Write your thoughts in the comments below!
